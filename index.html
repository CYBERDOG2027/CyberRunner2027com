THE UNIVERSE OF CYBER RUNNER 2027

In the year 2027, the world has become a dystopian megacity where corporations rule with absolute power. The line between human and machine has blurred beyond recognition, and the digital realm is just as real as the physical one.

Neon lights paint the perpetual night, while the hum of servers and the buzz of augmented reality implants create a constant background noise. The air is thick with the scent of ozone and synthetic pheromones.

You are a Runner - a digital mercenary navigating the dangerous streets and even more treacherous cyberspace. Your mission? Survival in a world that wants you dead or enslaved.

YOUR STORY

Once a promising netrunner for the megacorp Neo-Tokyo Systems, you discovered a secret that could bring down the entire corporate oligarchy. Now you're on the run, hunted by corporate assassins, rogue AIs, and rival runners.

Your only allies are the underground resistance movement "Ghost Echo" and your own augmented skills. But can you trust them? Can you even trust your own memories?

As you delve deeper into the conspiracy, you'll uncover truths about your past, the nature of reality, and the terrifying future that awaits if the corporations aren't stopped.

CYBERNETIC IMPLANTS

In 2027, the flesh is weak. Upgrade your body with these cutting-edge cybernetic enhancements:

  • NEURAL LINK

    Direct interface with computers and the net. Essential for any runner. Warning: may cause hallucinations and identity fragmentation.

  • REFLEX BOOSTER

    Overclock your nervous system for superhuman reaction times. Side effects include tremors and sensory overload.

  • OPTICAL CAMO

    Bend light around you for limited invisibility. Drains power quickly and doesn't hide thermal signatures.

  • PAIN EDITOR

    Selectively block pain receptors. Useful in combat, but may lead to unnoticed injuries and system damage.

  • MEMORY BANK

    Store and replay memories with perfect clarity. But are these really your memories, or were they planted?
